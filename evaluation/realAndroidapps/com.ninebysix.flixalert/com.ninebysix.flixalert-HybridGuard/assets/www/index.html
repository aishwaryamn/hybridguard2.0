FlixAlert let's you keep track of movies that are in-theaters, coming to theaters, or coming to DVD and Blu-Ray. With our notification system, you will always know when your movies are available!


With an account, you can tie your devices together and get notified no matter how you are connected. You can also choose from email and text msg notifications! Notifications are sent out the day of and a week prior to the movie's release date by default, but this can be changed under settings.


You can now also visit our site from anywhere! Check out www.flixalerts.com!
