Welcome to Clue Hunt, a game where you create your own scavenger hunt course in your house, backyard, or anywhere you want! Once you have set up a course, get your friends and family to play it, and see who can complete it in the fastest time!

Setting up a course involves:
create a clue - this can either be your own words or pick from a list of riddles.
Take a picture of the spot in the clue. Depending on the age and ability of your target audience, you can make the picture easy to locate (a panoramic view of an object or room) or difficult (close up of an object).
Lastly, write a code on a piece of paper and hide it at the clue spot. This code will be used to verify that someone has found your clue.
You can create as many checkpoints (clue and picture spots) as you like.

When you are finished, give your device to another person and see if they can complete your scavenger hunt course.

Have fun!
